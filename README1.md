Bio Template

Below is the sections you will talk about in your bio

Your Name: 
About Me: 
Likes: Dislikes: 
Strengths & Skills: 
Goals: Fun Fact: 
Favourite Quote: 
Image you want to include:


1. Your Name + Basic Info

You can include:

· Your name or preferred name

· Your year level or class

· A short tagline (e.g., “Digital Tech Student”, “Creative Thinker”, “Future Designer”)


2. About Me Paragraph

Write a short introduction about yourself, such as:

· Where you’re from

· A fun fact about you

· What you enjoy doing

· What you feel confident in

(1–3 sentences is enough.)


3. Likes

You can list things like:

· Your favourite foods

· Your hobbies

· Music, shows, or games you enjoy

· School subjects or topics you like

· Sports or activities you’re into


4. Dislikes

Keep this light and positive:

· Foods you don’t enjoy

· Things you find challenging

· Little annoyances or pet peeves you have


5. Your Strengths / Skills

Examples you might write about:

· Drawing or creative skills

· Are you part of a sports team

· What unique skills do you have

6. Your Goals

You can include:

· Something you want to learn this term

· A skill you want to improve

· A dream or ambition for the future

They don’t have to be DTC related

7. Fun or Random Facts

These make your bio more personal:

· Interesting hobbies

· Unique talents

· Special achievements

· Something surprising or fun about you


8. Favourite Quote (optional)

Add a school‑appropriate quote that inspires or motivates you.


9. Photos or Images

You might include:

· A profile picture (optional)

· An image that represents a hobby you enjoy

· A pattern, logo, or symbol you feel connected to
